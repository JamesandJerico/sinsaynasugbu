CALERUEGA

Caleruega Church, popularly known as the "Transfiguration Chapel," was established in 1995 in Batangas in Batulao, Brgy. Kaylaway in Nasugbu, Batangas, is a magnificent church built on top of a hill and surrounded by breathtaking surroundings.

The church is fairly modest, yet it has lovely architecture and a serene atmosphere. It is a great church for those seeking peace and tranquillity while admiring the beauty of the Church and environment.

It is a gorgeous and heavenly site for a wonderful wedding ceremony and is known as one of the most popular wedding churches near Tagaytay.

Caleruega is also a venue for retreats and reminiscence events, in addition to weddings. It is also an excellent location for prayer sessions, youth camps, religious gatherings, and conferences.
